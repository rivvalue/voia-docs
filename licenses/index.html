    VOÏA License Guide¶

    Understanding your account limits and license options.

    What is a License?¶

    Your VOÏA license determines: - How many campaigns you can run per year - Maximum number of participants per campaign - How many team members can use the platform - Which features are available - Support level

    Check your current license: Navigate to Settings → License Overview

    License Tiers¶

    Core License¶

    Best for: Small teams starting with customer feedback

    Limits: - 4 campaigns per year - 5 users maximum - 200 participants per campaign - 12-month duration

    Features included: - ✓ Traditional and conversational surveys - ✓ Basic analytics and NPS tracking - ✓ Email delivery - ✓ Standard support - ✓ Basic templates

    Pricing: Contact sales for pricing

    Plus License Popular¶

    Best for: Growing businesses with regular feedback needs

    Limits: - 4 campaigns per year - 10 users maximum - 2,000 participants per campaign - 12-month duration

    Features included: - ✓ Everything in Core - ✓ Advanced analytics and segmentation - ✓ Priority email support - ✓ Custom survey templates - ✓ API access - ✓ Executive PDF reports

    Pricing: Contact sales for pricing

    Pro License¶

    Best for: Enterprises with high-volume feedback programs

    Limits (Customizable): - 12+ campaigns per year (customizable) - 25+ users maximum (customizable) - 10,000+ participants per campaign (customizable) - 12+ month duration (customizable)

    Features included: - ✓ Everything in Plus - ✓ Custom analytics dashboards - ✓ Dedicated account manager - ✓ White-label branding - ✓ Custom integrations - ✓ SLA guarantee (99.9% uptime) - ✓ Advanced API access - ✓ Priority support (24/7)

    Pricing: Custom pricing based on your needs

    Trial License¶

    Best for: Evaluating VOÏA before committing

    Limits: - 1 campaign - 2 users maximum - 50 participants per campaign - 1-month duration

    Features included: - ✓ Basic platform features - ✓ Limited support - ✓ Sample survey templates

    Pricing: Free trial

    Understanding License Periods¶

    Annual License Cycle¶

    Licenses operate on an annual anniversary basis:

    Example: - License starts: January 15, 2025 - License renews: January 15, 2026 - Campaign limit resets: January 15, 2026

    Important: Campaign limits reset on your anniversary date, not January 1st

    License Expiration¶

    30 days before expiration:
    You'll receive renewal reminders

    Upon expiration: - You can still view existing data - Cannot create new campaigns - Cannot activate existing draft campaigns - Users cannot be added

    Renewal: Contact your account administrator or VOÏA sales

    License Usage Tracking¶

    How to Check Usage¶

    Navigate to Settings → License Overview to see: - Campaigns used this year (e.g., "3 of 4") - Current user count (e.g., "7 of 10") - Days remaining in license period - License expiration date

    Color indicators: - Green: Plenty of capacity remaining - Yellow: Approaching limit (80%+) - Red: At or over limit

    What Counts as "Usage"¶

    Campaigns: - Draft campaigns: Don't count - Active campaigns: Count toward limit - Completed campaigns: Count toward limit (for that year)

    Users: - Active users: Count toward limit - Deactivated users: Don't count - Invited (not yet accepted): Count toward limit

    Participants: - Counted per campaign (not total across all campaigns) - Adding 300 participants to one campaign uses 300 of your per-campaign limit

    Upgrading or Downgrading¶

    When to Upgrade¶

    You might need to upgrade if: - You hit your campaign limit mid-year - You need more users - You want advanced features (API access, white-label) - You need larger campaigns (more participants)

    Upgrade process: 1. Contact your account administrator 2. They contact VOÏA sales 3. Upgrade is processed within 24-48 hours 4. New limits apply immediately

    Downgrading¶

    Downgrades typically happen at renewal: - Cannot downgrade mid-cycle (except in special cases) - Existing campaigns continue running - New campaigns follow new limits

    Important: If downgrading reduces user limit below current user count, you'll need to deactivate users first

    License Enforcement¶

    Campaign Limit Reached¶

    What happens: - You cannot activate new campaigns - Draft campaigns stay in draft mode - Active campaigns continue normally

    Solutions: - Wait for annual renewal - Upgrade license - Delete draft campaigns (if not needed)

    User Limit Reached¶

    What happens: - Cannot invite new users - Existing users continue working normally

    Solutions: - Deactivate inactive users - Upgrade license - Wait for annual renewal

    Participant Limit Exceeded¶

    What happens per campaign: - Cannot add more participants to that campaign - Other campaigns unaffected

    Solutions: - Create a second campaign (if you have campaign quota) - Reduce participant list - Upgrade to higher participant limit

    Special License Types¶

    Demo Accounts¶

    Used for: VOÏA demonstrations and trials

    Limits: - Very limited (1 campaign, 50 participants) - Short duration - Not for production use

    To convert demo to full license: Contact VOÏA sales

    Custom Enterprise Licenses¶

    For: Large organizations with unique needs

    Can include: - Custom campaign limits (50+/year) - Custom user limits (100+) - Unlimited participants - Multi-year agreements - Volume discounts - Custom integrations

    Contact VOÏA sales for custom licensing

    Frequently Asked Questions¶

    Can I exceed my campaign limit temporarily?¶

    No, the system enforces limits strictly. Contact sales for emergency upgrades.

    What happens to my data if my license expires?¶

    Your data is preserved for 90 days after expiration. You can still log in and view data (read-only). Renew within 90 days to avoid data loss.

    Can I transfer campaigns from one license year to the next?¶

    No, campaigns are tied to the license year they were created in. A new year means fresh campaign quota.

    How do I know when I'm approaching my limits?¶

    VOÏA displays warnings when you reach 80% of any limit (campaigns, users, or participants).

    Can I buy additional campaigns mid-year?¶

    Yes, contact sales for campaign add-on packages. Pricing varies based on your current license.

    What if I deactivate a campaign - does it free up my quota?¶

    No, once a campaign is activated, it counts toward your annual limit even if you later close it.

    Do trial users count toward my user limit?¶

    Yes, anyone with access to your VOÏA account counts as a user.
