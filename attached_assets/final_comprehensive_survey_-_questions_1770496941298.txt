Q5. Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)
Overall, how satisfied are you with your experience with our company?
    [Scale: 1-5]
    1 = Very dissatisfied
    2 = Dissatisfied
    3 = Neutral
    4 = Satisfied
    5 = Very satisfied


Q6. Customer Effort Score (CES)
How easy was it to interact with our company and get your needs met?
    [Scale: 1-8]
    1 = Very difficult
    2 = Difficult
    3 = Somewhat difficult
    4 = Neutral
    5 = Somewhat easy
    6 = Easy
    7 = Very easy
    8 = Extremely easy
