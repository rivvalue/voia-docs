Email Delivery Modes

VOÏA-Managed:

  • Use platform's AWS SES credentials
  • Send from your verified domain
  • Requires DNS configuration (DKIM records)
  • White-labeled email delivery

Client-Managed:

  • Use your own SMTP server
  • Full control over credentials
  • Supports Gmail, AWS SES, SendGrid, etc.
  • No DNS configuration required
