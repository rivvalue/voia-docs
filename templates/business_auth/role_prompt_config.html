About Role Prompt Guidance

How Role Guidance Works:

  • 4-Tier Hierarchy: Campaign → Business Account → Platform → ROLE_METADATA defaults
  • Topic-Aware: Different guidance can be set per role+topic combination (e.g., Manager+Pricing vs Manager+Product)
  • Platform defaults apply to all accounts unless they configure their own overrides
  • This page configures the Platform tier (third level in hierarchy)
