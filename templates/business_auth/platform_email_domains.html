About Domain Management

Manual Domain Verification Workflow:

  1. Add domain to AWS SES Console - Log into AWS Console and add the business account's domain
  2. Copy DKIM records - AWS will generate 3 DKIM records (name/value pairs)
  3. Enter DKIM records here - Use this page to store the DKIM records for each business account
  4. Share with business account - Provide DKIM records to the business account for their DNS configuration
  5. Verify DNS - After business account configures DNS, verify in AWS Console
  6. Mark as verified - Once verified in AWS, update the status here
