About Platform Email Settings

VOÏA-Managed Email Delivery:

  • Business accounts can use the platform's AWS SES credentials for email sending
  • Emails are sent white-labeled from each business account's verified domain
  • Business accounts must verify their domains via DNS DKIM records
  • Manual workflow: You add domains to AWS Console and provide DKIM records to business accounts
  • No AWS SDK integration - fully manual domain management process
