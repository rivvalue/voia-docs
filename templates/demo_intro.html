Welcome to Your Voxa Demo

You're about to experience how Voxa turns open-ended feedback into actionable insight — in minutes.

In this simulation, you'll step into the role of a customer invited by Archelo Group, a growing workplace solutions provider, to share feedback on their existing product: ArcheloFlow.

🏢 About Archelo Group

Archelo Group helps mid-sized companies improve remote onboarding, internal communication, and employee engagement through its flagship platform, ArcheloFlow.

As part of their continuous improvement process, they've launched a feedback initiative using Rivvalue's VoC agent — Voxa — and you've been invited to participate.

💬 What You'll Do

  • Choose your preferred feedback method:
  • A modern, AI-powered conversational flow, or
  • A traditional, question-based survey
  • Answer a few quick questions, just like a real customer or employee would
  • Explore the live insights dashboard, instantly generated from your input

⚡ What You'll See, in real-time:

  • NPS score & distribution
  • Churn risk flags
  • Growth opportunities
  • Key sentiment & themes
  • Account-level takeaways

⏱️ What to Expect

  • Total time: Less than 2 minutes
  • No login, no spam, no setup
  • All responses are anonymized and used only for demonstration
