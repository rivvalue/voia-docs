## What is VOÏA?

VOÏA (Voice of Client) is a conversational Voice of Client (VoC) platform that collects unstructured customer feedback and converts it into structured, analysis-ready data.

It replaces traditional surveys with AI-driven conversations and provides clear insights into customer sentiment, loyalty, and risk.

### Key Benefits

<span class="badge badge-new">AI-Powered</span> **Smart Analysis**  
Automatically analyze customer feedback to identify trends, sentiment, and opportunities.

<span class="badge badge-new">Easy to Use</span> **Simple Campaign Creation**  
Create and send surveys to your customers in minutes, not hours.

<span class="badge badge-new">Conversational</span> **Natural Language Surveys**  
Let customers respond in their own words through AI-powered conversations.

<span class="badge badge-new">Actionable Insights</span> **Executive Reports**  
Get clear, visual reports that show exactly what matters to your business.

## Quick Start Guide

### 1. Create Your First Campaign

A campaign is a survey you send to your customers. Here's how to get started:

1. Click **Campaigns** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Campaign**
3. Choose a name (e.g., "Q1 2025 Customer Feedback")
4. Set start and end dates
5. Customize your survey questions

**Time needed:** 5-10 minutes

---

### 2. Add Participants

Participants are the customers who will receive your survey:

1. Navigate to **Participants**
2. Add individually or upload a CSV file
3. Include name, email, and any additional details

**Time needed:** 5 minutes (or instant with CSV import)

---

### 3. Send Invitations

Once your campaign is ready, send invitations to your participants:

1. Open your campaign
2. Click **Activate Campaign**
3. Invitations send automatically on your start date

**Automatic reminders:** VOÏA sends reminder emails automatically to boost response rates.

---

### 4. Review Your Results

Watch responses come in and analyze the results:

1. Go to your **Dashboard** for a real-time summary of NPS, sentiment, and response rate
2. Open your campaign and click **Insights** to explore the full tab-based analytics workspace — five tabs cover everything from high-level trends to individual account health
3. Download executive reports to share findings with leadership

**Updates:** Your dashboard and campaign analytics update in real-time as responses arrive. See the [Analytics & Business Intelligence section](features.md#analytics--business-intelligence) of the Feature Guide for a full explanation of each tab and report.

## Understanding Your Account

### User Roles

**Admin**  
Full access to create campaigns, manage users, and configure settings.

**Manager**  
Create and manage campaigns, add participants, view analytics.

**Viewer**  
View dashboards and reports only (cannot create or edit).

### License Tiers

Your account has specific limits based on your license:

- **Campaigns per year**
- **Maximum participants per campaign**
- **Number of users**
